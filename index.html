Project Overview

This project involved designing and developing a large-scale mosque complex in the Marat region of Riyadh. The scope included a mosque, school, retail shops, and residential villas for the Imam and Muazzin.

  • A central Mosque
  • A School
  • Multiple Retail Shops
  • Residential villas for the Imam and Muazzin
  • Ample parking areas

Design Concept

Inspired by the principles of Islamic geometry, the mosque design integrates symmetry, proportion, and harmony. Traditional Islamic motifs are reimagined using modern materials, creating a balance between heritage and contemporary aesthetics.

Architectural Features

The mosque incorporates sustainable design features such as natural ventilation, efficient shading, and careful orientation. The interiors are designed with a focus on light and spiritual ambiance, enhancing the prayer experience for worshippers.

My Role

As the lead AutoCAD Draftsman, I was responsible for:

  • Creating conceptual 3D visuals and renders
  • Preparing architectural drawings for submission
  • Developing detailed construction drawings for execution
